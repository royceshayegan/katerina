So, here’s my story...

In my early 20s, I landed in the States with $3 to my name.

I’d traveled from Far East Russia where my family lacked modern luxuries like hot water.

In my earliest years, life at home was unstable. So I found solace in a magical inner world, where anything is possible...

I’d hang out in the library reading Tolstoy, or in nature communing with the water brooks – connecting with Spirit through prayer, contemplating the shimmering water and the frequency of the sound it made.

The rustling leaves whispered of a divine soul purpose, and I felt inspired to uncover it. I wondered, “What’s it like beyond the borders of my little world?”

Fast forward a couple decades. I’m stepping off the airplane with a pocketful of crackers because I don’t know where my next meal is coming from.

My first job in the States was scrubbing floors. I had high hopes for something better, but that’s all that was available to start. Within a year, I found a sales position at a jewelry store – where I tasted financial abundance for the first time.

After a while, though, I felt stagnant and needed a lifestyle change. So I started spending time in the gym. Next thing I know, I’m preparing for a fitness modeling competition.

Over the next several years, relentless fitness took a toll on my body. Eventually I suffered adrenal blowout, so I was bedridden for days at a time. On top of that, I got a medical report that showed rapidly-multiplying precancerous cells in my body.

Over the next several months, I drank green juices, and ate raw, organic superfoods, salads, quinoa, nuts, you name it. And within a couple years, I hadn’t only made a complete recovery – I was overflowing with energy and vitality.

I still had trials and initiations ahead, but overcoming such a major health crisis gave me confidence. Like a phoenix rising from the ashes, I was bigger than my circumstances.

All that inspiration ultimately led to my first business. I wanted to leverage what I’d learned about health and vitality to make a difference in other people’s lives, too.

Since I didn’t know how to get a business off the ground, I became like a sponge – reading countless books, attending conferences, meeting like-minded people, and expanding my sense of what’s possible.

Then one day at a conference, everything clicked. I’d already been to so many conferences, and I’d heard similar messages many times before; but in this pivotal moment, I had a different quality of listening…

“Do you wait, or do you create?”

All of a sudden, something shifted at the core. The question resonated so powerfully that it opened up a vision of my highest purpose – the only thing that could bring true fulfillment.

I realized I was on planet Earth to help liberate souls. And I felt the urgency to surrender to this assignment. It was time to let my heart lead the way… It was time to CREATE!
